Privacy Policy — Fajar

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Fajar is a free Islamic prayer times app. This privacy policy explains clearly and honestly what information the app accesses, how it is used, and what it never does.

The short version: Fajar uses your device's GPS to calculate accurate prayer times for your location. That is the only data it ever accesses. It is never sent to a server, never stored anywhere, and never shared with anyone.

What information Fajar accesses

Location

Fajar requests permission to access your device's location. This is the only data the app ever accesses.

Why: Prayer times are calculated based on your geographical coordinates (latitude and longitude). Without knowing your location, accurate prayer times cannot be determined.

How it is used: Your coordinates are sent directly from your device to the Aladhan API (aladhan.com) to retrieve prayer times calculated for your specific location. This request happens on your device and the response is displayed to you. No copy of your location is stored anywhere by Fajar.

What it is not used for: Your location is never stored on any server operated by us. It is never sold. It is never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party beyond the prayer time API request described above.

Third-party services

Fajar uses two external APIs to provide its content. When the app makes a request to these services, your device's IP address may be visible to them as part of how the internet works. Neither service is given your name, email, or any identifying information by Fajar.

Aladhan API (aladhan.com)

Used to calculate Islamic prayer times based on your location coordinates. Please refer to aladhan.com for their own privacy practices.

AlQuran Cloud API (alquran.cloud)

Used to display the daily Quranic verse. No location data is sent with this request. Please refer to alquran.cloud for their own privacy practices.

What Fajar does not collect

  • No name, email address, or any account information — Fajar has no accounts
  • No usage analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No browsing history
  • No contact list, camera, microphone, or any other device data
  • No payment information — Fajar is completely free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions

Data storage

All settings you configure in Fajar (calculation method, notification preferences, display preferences) are stored only on your device using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Children

Fajar does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Since no personal data is collected at all, the app is appropriate for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes in a meaningful way, the updated policy will be made available at the same URL and the "Last updated" date at the top of this document will be revised. Continued use of the app after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

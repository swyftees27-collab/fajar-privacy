Privacy Policy

Fajar · Last updated May 2026

I want to be straightforward with you about how Fajar works and what it does with your information. The honest answer is: very little. Fajar uses your device's GPS to figure out where you are so it can calculate accurate prayer times. That's it. Your location is never stored, never sent to any server we operate, and never shared with anyone.

Location

Fajar asks for permission to access your location because prayer times depend on where you are in the world. Without your coordinates, the app simply can't do its job.

When you open the app, your device's coordinates are used to make a request to the Aladhan API at aladhan.com, which returns the prayer times for your area. That request goes from your phone directly to their service. We don't sit in the middle, we don't log it, and we don't keep a copy of where you are.

Your location is never stored on any server we run. It is never sold. It is never passed to advertisers or data brokers. The only thing that happens with it is the prayer time calculation described above.

The two services Fajar uses

Fajar relies on two external APIs to deliver its content. When your device contacts these services, your IP address is naturally visible to them — that's just how the internet works. But Fajar sends them no personal information about you beyond the coordinates needed to calculate prayer times.

The Aladhan API at aladhan.com handles the prayer time calculations. The AlQuran Cloud API at alquran.cloud provides the daily Quranic verse shown on the home screen — no location data is sent for that request at all. Both services have their own privacy policies you can read on their respective websites.

What we don't collect

Fajar has no accounts, no login, and no backend of its own. There is nothing to collect even if we wanted to. Specifically, the app collects no names, no email addresses, no usage statistics, no crash reports, no advertising identifiers, no browsing history, and no payment information. The app is completely free with no purchases or subscriptions of any kind.

Your settings

Everything you configure in Fajar — your calculation method, notification preferences, display settings — is saved directly on your device using local storage. None of it leaves your phone and none of it is accessible to us.

Children

Since Fajar collects no personal information from anyone, it is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly gather data from children or anyone else.

If this policy changes

If something meaningful changes in how Fajar handles your information, the updated policy will be posted at this same URL and the date at the top will be updated. Continuing to use the app after a change means you're comfortable with the updated terms.

Questions

If anything here is unclear or you have a concern, feel free to reach out at fajar.prayertimes@gmail.com. I'm happy to answer.

Fajar is built with the sole intention of serving the Muslim community. Jazakum Allahu subḥānahu wataʿālā Khayran.
